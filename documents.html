Preparation Tips

📸 Photograph Tips

Use a plain white background. Ensure proper lighting. Wear formal attire. Size: 3.5cm × 4.5cm recommended.

📝 SOP Writing Tips

Keep it concise (500-800 words). Highlight academic goals, achievements, and why you deserve the scholarship.

✍️ Resume Tips

Use a clean format. Include education, skills, projects, and achievements. Keep it to 1-2 pages max.

📂 Document Organization

Keep all documents scanned in PDF format. Name files clearly (e.g., Marksheet_10th_YourName.pdf). Maintain both digital and physical copies.
