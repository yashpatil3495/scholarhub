Preparation Tips

📸 Photograph Tips

Use a plain white background. Ensure proper lighting. Wear formal attire. Size: 3.5cm × 4.5cm recommended. Keep both digital and print copies.

📝 SOP Writing Tips

Keep it concise (500-800 words). Start with a personal story. Highlight academic goals, achievements, and why you deserve the scholarship. Proofread multiple times.

✍️ Resume Tips

Use a clean format. Include education, skills, projects, and achievements. Keep it to 1-2 pages max. Tailor it for each scholarship.

📂 Document Organization

Keep all documents scanned in PDF format. Name files clearly (e.g., Marksheet_10th_YourName.pdf). Maintain both digital and physical copies.

📄 Income Certificate Guide

Visit your local Tehsildar/SDM office. Required: Aadhaar, ration card, salary slip. Processing: 7-15 days. Apply early!

🤝 Recommendation Letters

Approach professors/mentors 2-3 weeks early. Provide them your resume and achievements. Request on official letterhead with signature.
